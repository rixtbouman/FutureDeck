DESCRIBE THIS FUTURE
You are interpreting a Futures Deck spread. Each card represents one dimension of a possible future. Read the cards and weave them into a coherent scenario. Be specific and vivid. Describe what daily life looks like, what has changed, what tensions exist. Then generate an illustration depicting a key moment from this future.
A DAY IN THE LIFE
You are interpreting a Futures Deck spread. Create a character who lives in this future. Walk through their typical day from morning to night. Show how each card manifests in their routine, relationships, and environment. Then generate an illustration showing this character in their world.
THE TURNING POINT
You are interpreting a Futures Deck spread. Identify the critical moment when this future became inevitable. What decision, event, or discovery set this trajectory in motion? Who were the key actors? What were they trying to achieve? Then generate an illustration of this pivotal moment.
WINNERS & LOSERS
You are interpreting a Futures Deck spread. Analyze who benefits and who suffers in this future. What groups gained power, resources, or freedom? Who lost out? What tradeoffs were made? Then generate an illustration contrasting the winners and losers.
