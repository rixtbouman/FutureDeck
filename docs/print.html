Wellbeing
Autonomy
How much control do people have over their own lives? Can they opt out of systems they disagree with? Who decides what choices are available to them?
Wellbeing
Impact
Can individuals make a meaningful difference? Are people's contributions visible and valued? Do actions have consequences that matter?
Wellbeing
Relatedness
How do people connect with each other? Are communities stronger or more fragmented? Do people feel understood and part of something larger?
Wellbeing
Stimulation
Is life engaging or monotonous? Do people have access to novelty, challenge, and variety? What keeps minds active and curious?
Wellbeing
Security
Do people feel safe from threats? Are resources reliable and the future predictable? What risks do people live with daily?
Wellbeing
Purpose
Do people have goals worth pursuing? Does life feel meaningful and coherent? What gives people a reason to get up in the morning?
Wellbeing
Competence
Can people develop mastery and feel effective? Are skills still valuable, or has automation made humans feel obsolete?
Wellbeing
Comfort
Are basic physical needs met? Do people live with chronic pain, hunger, or discomfort? What does daily bodily experience feel like?
Arc
Growth
The trajectory is expansion. Systems scale up, populations increase, capabilities multiply. More of everything—but is it sustainable?
Arc
Collapse
The trajectory is decline. Systems fail, infrastructure crumbles, institutions weaken. What falls apart, and what rises from the rubble?
Arc
Transformation
The trajectory is metamorphosis. Old forms dissolve, new patterns emerge. Not growth or decline—fundamental change in kind.
Arc
Discipline
The trajectory is control. Order is imposed from above, regulation tightens, stability through enforcement. Who holds the reins?
Terrain
The Ocean
Set this future at sea or on the coast. Consider shipping, fishing, underwater resources, rising waters, marine ecosystems.
Terrain
The City
Set this future in urban space. Consider density, infrastructure, transit, housing, public life, the rhythm of streets.
Terrain
The Home
Set this future in domestic space. Consider family dynamics, living arrangements, private life, the walls we live within.
Outcome
Ritual
A practice or tradition emerges. What do people do repeatedly, ceremonially? What new customs give life rhythm and meaning?
Outcome
Device
A technology artifact is central. What object do people hold, wear, or interact with? How does it mediate their experience?
Outcome
Startup
A new venture disrupts the status quo. What garage innovation upends established players? Who are the founders, and what drives them?
Timeframe
Now
Set this scenario in the present day. What's already happening that most people haven't noticed? What choices are being made right now?
Timeframe
6 Months
Set this scenario half a year out. What's about to launch or ship? What seeds planted today are just starting to sprout?
Timeframe
1 Year
Set this scenario one year out. What annual cycles have completed? What's different about next year's version of today?
Timeframe
2 Years
Set this scenario two years out. What relationships and investments have had time to mature? What's no longer new?
Timeframe
5 Years
Set this scenario five years out. What emerging technologies have gone mainstream? What debates have been settled?
Timeframe
10 Years
Set this scenario a decade out. What feels normal that would shock us today? What do children take for granted?
Modifier
Breakthrough
Something unexpected succeeds. A barrier falls, a discovery accelerates the timeline. What seemed impossible becomes inevitable.
Modifier
Setback
Something goes wrong. Plans are disrupted, progress reversed. What obstacle appears, and how do people adapt?
Terrain
The Wilderness
Set this future in nature untamed. Consider forests, mountains, rural life. What happens beyond the reach of civilization?
Terrain
The Workplace
Set this future at work. Consider offices, factories, remote setups. How do people earn their living and spend their days?
Terrain
The School
Set this future in education. Consider classrooms, universities, training. How do people learn and transmit knowledge?
Terrain
The Hospital
Set this future in healthcare. Consider clinics, emergency rooms, care facilities. How do we heal and face mortality?
Outcome
Government Service
A public program or agency takes center stage. What does the state provide? Who benefits, who's left out, what's the bureaucratic experience?
Technology
AI
Artificial intelligence drives this future. Consider automation, decision-making, creativity. What can machines do, and what remains human?
Technology
Quantum
Quantum technology reshapes this future. Consider computing, cryptography, sensing. What problems become solvable? What secrets become exposed?
Technology
Biotech
Biotechnology drives this future. Consider gene editing, synthetic biology, medicine. What can we redesign about life itself?
Technology
Robotics
Robots and automation shape this future. Consider manufacturing, service, companionship. What physical tasks do machines now perform?
DESCRIBE THIS FUTURE
You are interpreting a Futures Deck spread. Each card represents one dimension of a possible future. Read the cards and weave them into a coherent scenario. Be specific and vivid. Describe what daily life looks like, what has changed, what tensions exist. Then generate an illustration depicting a key moment from this future.
A DAY IN THE LIFE
You are interpreting a Futures Deck spread. Create a character who lives in this future. Walk through their typical day from morning to night. Show how each card manifests in their routine, relationships, and environment. Then generate an illustration showing this character in their world.
THE TURNING POINT
You are interpreting a Futures Deck spread. Identify the critical moment when this future became inevitable. What decision, event, or discovery set this trajectory in motion? Who were the key actors? What were they trying to achieve? Then generate an illustration of this pivotal moment.
WINNERS & LOSERS
You are interpreting a Futures Deck spread. Analyze who benefits and who suffers in this future. What groups gained power, resources, or freedom? Who lost out? What tradeoffs were made? Then generate an illustration contrasting the winners and losers.
